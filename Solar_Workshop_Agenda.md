# ☀️ Solar Panel Installation Workshop Agenda

## Event Overview
- **Title:** Solar Panel Installation Workshop
- **Date:** February 15, 2025
- **Host:** Sarah Johnson
- **Location:** Green Energy Community Center

---

## 📅 Schedule

### 1. Morning Session: Theory (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
* **Introduction to Photovoltaics:** Understanding how solar cells convert sunlight to electricity.
* **System Components:** Panels, Inverters, Charge Controllers, and Battery Storage.
* **Safety Protocol:** Electrical safety and rooftop working guidelines.

### 2. Lunch Break (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM)
* Networking with local solar enthusiasts.

### 3. Afternoon Session: Hands-on (1:00 PM - 3:00 PM)
* **Mounting Basics:** Securing rails and flashings.
* **Wiring Lab:** Series vs. Parallel connections.
* **Testing:** Using a multimeter to verify output.

---

1. Impact Driver
2. Wire Strippers
